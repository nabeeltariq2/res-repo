Instructions

  • Click on My Movie List. This contains a list of movies you can explore for further details.

  • Click on each movie to view further details and rate it out of a score of 5 based on your liking. Take your time to explore each movie and rate it based on your opinion. The more movies you rate, the better the recommendations you will get.

  • Once you have rated all the movies you want, click on Calculate & Show Recommendations. It will take 2-3 minutes for the next page to load, so you can do anything else in the meanwhile. Don't click on anything else till the page loads.

  • The next pages will display your recommendations, and some questions related to those that you may need to answer. Take your time to explore each recommended movie (click on it for further details) and then answer the relevant questions.

  • That's it for one session! Explore and rate more/different movies to get more recommendations. Tell us how they differ from the previous ones!

  • THINGS to NOTE:

  • Please complete all the questions in one single session, otherwise the recommendations will reset! Movies you rated will be saved though.

  • Please wait for every page to completely load before using it. You can see the loading status on the browser tab name.

  • That's it!Enjoy!!

In case of any queries/issues/additional feedback, please email movirec_movies@gmail.com
